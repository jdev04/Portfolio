Why It Matters

A Slowly
Dying Earth

Carbon Emissions has a ripple affect on our environment it melts our polar ice caps, raises sea levels and disturbs animals natural habitats which can throw off entire ecosystems

The Problem With Climate Change

Climates all over the world have been changing slowly because of our carbon emissions 2020 being the second hottest year recorded by the NOAA's Data on the average hottest temperature

The Rising
Death Toll

A study found in 2018 that more than 8 million people (Roughly the same amount of people as the whole of New York City) died to carbon and fossil fuel emissions alone

The Greenhouse
Effect

Along with Climate Change the Greenehouse Effect is when the sun hits earth warming the surface but because of the rising C02 in the atmosphere it is not able to return back to space
